Blocking Guidelines
When to Block:
  • Scheduled maintenance
  • Renovation or repairs
  • Special events or functions
  • Cleaning or deep cleaning
  • Safety inspections
  • Equipment installation
Best Practices:
  • Block for the minimum required time
  • Provide clear, specific reasons
  • Consider exam schedules
  • Notify affected parties in advance
  • Update venue status after completion
