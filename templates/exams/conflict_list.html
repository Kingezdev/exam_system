Conflict Resolution Guide
Time Overlap Conflicts

When exams are scheduled at the same time for the same students. Resolution options:

  • Reschedule one of the exams to a different time
  • Change the exam date if possible
  • Split the exam into multiple sessions
Same Students Conflicts

When students have multiple exams scheduled simultaneously. Resolution options:

  • Check if students are actually enrolled in both courses
  • Coordinate with departments to resolve scheduling
  • Consider alternative assessment methods
Venue Conflicts

When multiple exams are scheduled in the same venue. Resolution options:

  • Reallocate one exam to a different venue
  • Check venue availability and capacity
  • Consider using multiple smaller venues
Invigilator Conflicts

When invigilators are assigned to multiple exams simultaneously. Resolution options:

  • Reassign invigilators based on availability
  • Check invigilator workload limits
  • Consider backup invigilator assignments
